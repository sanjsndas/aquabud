Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve all five boroughs of New York City (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island) as well as parts of Long Island and Westchester County. Please contact us with your specific location to confirm service availability in your area.

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

For standard service requests, we typically respond within 24-48 hours. Emergency services (such as irrigation system leaks) receive priority and we aim to respond within 4-6 hours during business hours. Response times may vary based on current workload and seasonal demand.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, we provide free estimates for all our services. Our team will visit your property to assess your needs and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote. For smaller projects, we can often provide estimates remotely based on photos and descriptions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), checks, and cash. For larger projects, we also offer financing options through our partners with flexible payment plans.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. AquaBud is fully licensed, bonded, and insured to operate in New York State. Our team carries comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We'd be happy to provide certificates of insurance upon request.

What is your service guarantee?

We stand behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our services, we'll make it right at no additional cost to you. Our irrigation systems come with a 2-year warranty, and our landscaping work is guaranteed for one full growing season.
